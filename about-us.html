Who We are

Delicious Authentic Clay Pot Biryani - Each Biryani is made to order and cooked in individual clay pots which brings out the earthly and natural flavors infused with Indian spices. One of a kind and treat to your taste buds. Every week will be a different menu and the menu will be published on this group. For now we are only delivering Pot Biryanis for Friday Evening and Potless Biryani on Saturday Lunch, we are planning to expand soon.

Pot biryani, a tantalizing Indian dish, layers fragrant basmati rice with succulent meats or vegetables, infused with a symphony of spices. Slow-cooked in a sealed pot, it traps the flavors, creating a harmonious explosion with every bite. Garnished with caramelized onions, fresh herbs, and a squeeze of lime, it's a feast fit for royalty.
Introducing our tantalizing Pot Biryani, a signature dish at our restaurant! Prepared with fragrant basmati rice, aromatic spices, and tender marinated meat or vegetables, it's slow-cooked to perfection in a traditional clay pot, infusing every grain with rich flavors. Served piping hot, each spoonful promises a delightful blend of spices and textures, sure to transport your taste buds on a culinary journey. Experience the essence of authentic Indian cuisine with our Pot Biryani, a feast for all the senses.
